Welcome to My E-Commerce Website

E-commerce, or electronic commerce, refers to the buying and selling of goods and services over the internet.
Rather than conducting transactions in a physical store, e-commerce enables businesses to sell their products and services on websites and mobile apps.

E-commerce leverages the reach, convenience and capabilities of the internet to provide enhanced benefits
for retailers and more choice and service for customers. It enables a scalable, cost-effective model for selling globally.
